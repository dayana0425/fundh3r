Our Mission

Enable Crypto Donations to Support Womens Reproductive Rights

Onboarding non-profits to diversify their funding options and localizing source for organizations. We believe the goal of crypto is to support freedom. Women must have the freedom to realize their full potential professionally and personally. This can only be done if women have the right to choose if and when they want to be mothers.
